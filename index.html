Dominion Oasis

Dominion Oasis is a prototype expansion which would have been the 17th Dominion Expansion. This set introduces Ace cards and a custom Unique deck with the core of the expansion relying on Betting. There are 10 Kingdom cards, 1 card not in the supply, and 10 Unique deck cards. The expansion icon is .


Dominion Oasis was originally planned to be Dominion Saloon, following the wild-west theming. Saloon stayed as an Action - Ace card.
